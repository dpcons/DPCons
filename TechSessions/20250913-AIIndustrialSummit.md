# Azure IoT Operations: From Vision to Revolution
## Sep 13rd 2025 - AI Industrial Summit - Sofia 
### Abstract
Azure IoT Operations is more than just a platform—it's an evolving ecosystem that's redefining how we manage and scale IoT operations. In this session, we’ll take you on a journey through the present and future of Azure IoT capabilities, with a focus on the latest features.
From advanced device management to enhanced security, we'll explore the tangible benefits these innovations bring to real-world scenarios. Join us to discover how Azure is shaping the next generation of tech solutions with a mix of practicality, automation, and a splash of geeky magic.
